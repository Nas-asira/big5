About us

ABOUT US

BIG 5 TOURISM GROUP LTD is a Tourism & Travel Company based in Kampala, Uganda.

BIG 5 TOURISM GROUP LTD is a Tourism & Travel Company based in Kampala, Uganda. We offer you a large range of services to the four corners of the earth to experience travel to the fullest. Created in 2010 by George Milton Mwesigwa, a young Ugandan entrepreneur, Big 5 Tourism Group focuses on Student excursions throughout the FOUR continents as our niche.

In the beginning of 2013, after a successful trip to Paris – France, Big 5 developed into a more international platform permitting to clients all over the world to design with us the tours, safaris, and trips of their dreams. We, at Big 5 take great pride in partnering with local guides in every destination we offer to make sure our clients, YOU, have the safest and most authentic experience possible.
